SOS (Creamed Hamburger Gravy)

Description

This dish was a staple in my family when I was a kid up until my father who was a veteran passed away. I enjoyed it every time he made it, even if it wasn't the healthiest thing to eat.

Obviously gravy isn't that healthy, and beef has had a bad link to heart disease.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cubes chicken bouillon, crumbled
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups low-fat milk

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat; stir in the ground beef, chicken bouillion, garlic powder, black pepper, and onion powder. Cook and stir until the beef is crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink, about 7 minutes
  2. Stir in the flour and cook for 3 minutes, stirring constantly. Stir in the milk and bring to a simmer, stirring constantly. Reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer 5 minutes to thicken.
